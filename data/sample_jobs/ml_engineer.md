# Machine Learning Engineer (Platform)

We're hiring an ML engineer to build the infrastructure around our models: serving,
evaluation, and the data pipelines that feed them. This is a platform and systems role
more than a modeling role; you'll spend more time making models reliable in production
than training new ones.

## What you'll do
- Build and operate model-serving infrastructure: low-latency inference, versioning,
  rollout, and monitoring for drift and regressions.
- Own evaluation harnesses so we can tell whether a change actually helped.
- Build data pipelines that are reproducible and debuggable.
- Partner with researchers to get their work into production without it falling over.

## What we're looking for
- Strong software engineering fundamentals. This is an engineering role first.
- Experience deploying ML systems, or strong backend/infra experience plus a real
  interest in ML.
- Comfort with Python, containers, and cloud infrastructure.
- Familiarity with the practical failure modes of ML in production: data drift,
  training/serving skew, silent quality regressions.

## Nice to have
- Experience with vector databases, embeddings, or retrieval systems.
- A background that blends research and engineering.
