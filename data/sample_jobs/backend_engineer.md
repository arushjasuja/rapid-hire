# Senior Backend Engineer

We're a small team building the payments and billing platform behind a growing SaaS
product. You'd own services that move real money, which means correctness and
observability aren't optional.

## What you'll do
- Design and build HTTP APIs that other teams depend on: billing, invoicing, usage
  metering.
- Own the data model for parts of the billing system: schema, migrations, and the
  performance of the queries that hit it.
- Keep the services healthy in production. You'll be on call for what you build, with
  the tooling and support to do it sanely.
- Work closely with a couple of other backend engineers; review each other's code and
  design decisions.

## What we're looking for
- Several years writing backend services in a language like Python, Go, or Java. We
  write mostly Python.
- Real experience designing APIs and relational schemas, and reasoning about
  performance when things get slow.
- Comfort operating services: metrics, logging, on-call, the usual failure modes of
  networked systems.
- A habit of testing your work and writing things down clearly.
- Bonus: payments, billing, or other domains where getting the numbers wrong is
  expensive.

## What we don't care about
- Where (or whether) you got a degree.
- Whether you already know our exact stack. We assume good engineers ramp quickly.
